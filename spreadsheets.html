Treatment Spreadsheets

Links to spreadsheets summarizing treatment outcomes for various auditory conditions, including hyperacusis, noxacusis, middle ear myoclonus (MEM), tonic tensor tympani syndrome (TTTS), and tinnitus, can be found below. These spreadsheets compile data from patient reports, clinical observations, and published studies to provide an overview of how different therapies have performed across cases.

They are intended as informational resources to help readers better understand which interventions have shown potential benefit, which remain experimental, and where evidence is still limited. Each spreadsheet is organized by condition and treatment type for easier comparison and reference.
