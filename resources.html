Resources

Hyperacusis Guide is dedicated to helping individuals affected by hyperacusis — a rare and often misunderstood condition that causes an increased sensitivity to everyday sounds. Because of its rarity and the lack of widespread awareness, reliable information and supportive resources can be difficult to find. This website aims to bring together credible guidance, medical insights, and personal strategies to make navigating hyperacusis easier for those seeking understanding and relief.
